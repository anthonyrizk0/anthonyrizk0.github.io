Pomodoro Noodle

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 13, 2026

Short version: Pomodoro Noodle does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you create in the app stays on your device.

1. Who We Are

Pomodoro Noodle is developed and published by Anthony Rizk ("I", "me", "my"). If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach me at support@anthonyrizk.me.

2. Data I Collect

I collect no personal data. Pomodoro Noodle has no accounts, no sign-in, and no network calls that send your information anywhere. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected.
  • No usage analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are included in the app.
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking pixels are used.
  • No location data is accessed.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Completed session records (phase, duration, timestamps)
  • Notes you write during or after sessions
  • Your timer and preference settings
  • In-progress session state (persisted so the app can resume after restart)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or back it up yourself (e.g. via an iCloud device backup initiated by iOS/macOS). I have no access to it.

4. Third-Party Services

Pomodoro Noodle does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

The app is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain diagnostic and usage information as part of the App Store platform. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

Pomodoro Noodle does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages in this regard.

6. Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change in the future (for example, if optional cloud sync is added), this policy will be updated before those changes ship, and the effective date above will be revised. Continued use of the app after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

Questions or concerns about this privacy policy? Email me at support@anthonyrizk.me.
