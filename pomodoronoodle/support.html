Frequently Asked Questions

Why did my session history disappear?

Session history is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app or restoring your device without a backup will erase local data. iCloud sync is not currently supported.

The Live Activity isn't showing up on my Lock Screen.

Make sure Live Activities are enabled for Pomodoro Noodle in Settings → [Device Name] → Pomodoro Noodle → Live Activities. Your device must be running iOS 18.0 or later.

Siri / Shortcuts aren't working.

Open the Shortcuts app and look for Pomodoro Noodle actions. If they don't appear, try removing and reinstalling the app. Make sure Siri is enabled in Settings.

I didn't receive a notification when my timer finished.

Check that notifications are allowed for Pomodoro Noodle in Settings → Notifications → Pomodoro Noodle and that Do Not Focus / Focus mode isn't blocking them.
